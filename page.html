WELCOME FIGHT AGAINST VIOLENCE

Fight against

violence against women

You are not alone!

Violence against women occurs everywhere; in the family sphere, in the public space or even in the professional environment. In order to help women victims of violence, the CIDFF multidisciplinary teams offer legal information, psychological and/or social support and referral to partner structures, free of charge and confidentially. For advice, help and protection, contact your CIDFF!

In short

What violence?

The CIDFF supports women who are victims of all forms of violence: physical, sexual, psychological, administrative, economic, cyberviolence, etc.

For who ?

For all women victims of violence.

Or ?

98 CIDFF are present throughout the national territory and in overseas territories . They also run hotlines in local information centers in urban and rural areas .

How ?

By making an appointment with the CIDFF closest to you 

FIND MY CIDFF

We are here for you !

For more than 15 years, the CIDFF have been involved in the fight against violence against women. Partners of the public authorities, the CIDFF notably support women beneficiaries of protection systems and measures (serious danger telephone, anti-reconciliation bracelet, protection order). The CIDFF also conduct training and awareness sessions for the general public ( particularly within schools and higher education establishments ) but also for health professionals , justice professionals , law enforcement forces , police personnel businesses , etc. in order to prevent all forms of violence or sexist behavior .

You are a victim of violence, your CIDFF informs you of your rights and supports you.

98 centers present in mainland France and overseas are there to welcome you .
